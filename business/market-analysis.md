# BoomerBill Market Analysis

## Category
Personal time-value tracking for invisible labor and boundary management.

## Tailwinds
- Growing creator/freelancer focus on time economics.
- Rising privacy demand for local-first tools.
- Fatigue with account-heavy productivity products.

## Headwinds
- Low urgency compared to billing/time tracking incumbents.
- Habit retention can drop after initial novelty.

## Competitor Archetypes
- Generic time trackers.
- Freelance invoicing suites.
- Habit/journal apps.
- Productivity dashboards.

## White Space
An awareness-first tool focused on unpaid technical labor, not invoicing.

## Validation Data to Collect
- Time to first value from quickstart.
- Week-2 and week-4 retention cohorts.
- Hosted convenience willingness-to-pay range.
