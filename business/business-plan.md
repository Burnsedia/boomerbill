# BoomerBill Business Plan

## Product Role
BoomerBill is a local-first app that tracks unpaid tech support time and converts it to visible cost.

## ICP
- Primary: freelancers, developers, and IT helpers who repeatedly provide unpaid tech support.
- Secondary: creators and consultants setting personal boundaries around time.

## FOSS and Monetization Boundary
- Free forever: AGPL local app, core tracking, local storage, basic reporting.
- Paid: hosted sync, backups, team/shared reporting, priority support.

## Business Model
- Open-core trust funnel to hosted convenience.
- Low-ticket monthly plan for individuals, higher tier for teams.

## Primary KPI
- Week-4 retained active users.

## 90-Day Objectives
- Launch hosted beta with clear onboarding docs.
- Convert first 10 paid hosted users.
- Publish weekly changelog and monthly product recap.
